Congratulations

Join us in Recognizing Minister Emerson Tan

We are thrilled to recognize one of our own as our Church Member of the Month! Minister Tan has been an integral part of our church family, consistently demonstrating kindness, compassion, and generosity to those around them. Throughout his tenure, Mr. Tan has consistently demonstrated exceptional leadership, compassion, and commitment to the well-being of our congregation. His unwavering passion for spiritual growth, community service, and outreach has inspired countless individuals to live out their faith with greater purpose and meaning. We would like to take this opportunity to express our heartfelt appreciation for all that Minister Tan has done for us, both within and outside the church. We recognize his hard work, his compassion, and his unwavering commitment to serving others, and we are grateful for his presence in our lives.
