Join the Team

Are you looking for a community that will support and encourage you on your spiritual journey? Do you want to grow in your faith with like-minded individuals who share your passions and values? Joining one of our Groups is the perfect opportunity to do just that! Our groups are designed to provide a safe and welcoming space for people from all walks of life to come together, share their experiences, and learn from each other. Whether you're new to Christianity or have been following Jesus for years, we invite you to join us on this journey.

From Bible studies to practical life applications, our S4C Church Groups offer a wide range of activities that will help you deepen your understanding of God's Word and apply it to everyday life. Our groups are made up of people from all ages and backgrounds, so you'll find friends who understand what you're going through and can relate to your experiences. We also have groups for different stages of life, including students, young adults, families, and seniors, so there's something for everyone. By joining one of our Groups, you'll become part of a vibrant community that will support and encourage you every step of the way. Come and discover the joy of belonging to a church family!
