Join the Team

Are you looking for a community that will support and encourage you on your spiritual journey? Do you want to grow in your faith with like-minded individuals who share your passions and values? Joining one of our Groups is the perfect opportunity to do just that! Our groups are designed to provide a safe and welcoming space for people from all walks of life to come together, share their experiences, and learn from each other. Whether you're new to Christianity or have been following Jesus for years, we invite you to join us on this journey.

From Bible studies to practical life applications, our S4C Church Groups offer a wide range of activities that will help you deepen your understanding of God's Word and apply it to everyday life. Our groups are made up of people from all ages and backgrounds, so you'll find friends who understand what you're going through and can relate to your experiences. We also have groups for different stages of life, including students, young adults, families, and seniors, so there's something for everyone. By joining one of our Groups, you'll become part of a vibrant community that will support and encourage you every step of the way. Come and discover the joy of belonging to a church family!


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Security.

As we strive to serve our community, consider volunteering for our Ushering and Security ministry at S4C. By giving your time and talents, you can help create a warm and welcoming environment for worshipers of all ages. Our team is dedicated to ensuring the safety and comfort of our congregation, and we rely on volunteers like you to make it happen. Whether you're able to serve in the frontlines or behind the scenes, every contribution counts and makes a meaningful difference. By serving together, we can build a stronger, more loving community that reflects God's love for all people.

Kids Church.

At S4C, our Kids Church ministry is dedicated to providing a fun and safe environment where children can grow in their faith and learn valuable life skills. As a volunteer, you'll play a crucial role in helping us achieve this mission by leading small groups, teaching lessons, or assisting with activities. Our kids are the future leaders of our world, and we want to equip them with the tools they need to succeed - both spiritually and academically. By volunteering in Kids Church, you'll have the opportunity to make a lasting impact on these young lives and watch them grow into confident, compassionate, and courageous individuals. Join us in serving the next generation of Christians and helping shape the leaders of tomorrow!

Music Team.

At S4C, our Music Team is the heartbeat of our worship services, and we need your talent to help us make music that glorifies God. Whether you're a singer, guitarist, drummer, or piano player, we invite you to join our team and use your gift to serve others. As a member of the Music Team, you'll have the opportunity to create a spiritual atmosphere that lifts hearts, inspires souls, and brings people together in worship. By volunteering with us, you'll not only be using your musical talents for God's glory, but also being part of a community that shares your passion for music and ministry. Together, let's make music that makes a difference in the lives of our congregation and beyond!

Prayer Team.

At S4C, our Prayer Team is dedicated to lifting up our congregation, community, and world in prayer, seeking God's guidance, healing, and transformation. As a volunteer on our team, you'll have the opportunity to pray for others, experience spiritual growth, and be part of a community that seeks to honor God with their lives. Whether it's praying over a specific need or simply being present in prayer, every role is essential to helping us fulfill our purpose. By joining our Prayer Team, you'll not only be making a tangible difference in the lives of others, but also experiencing the power of prayer and connection that brings people together. Let's work together to create a spiritual atmosphere where prayers are lifted up to God, seeking His will and provision for all.

Administrative Support.

At S4C, our Administrative Support team is the backbone of our operations, providing essential behind-the-scenes support that enables us to fulfill our mission and serve our community. As a volunteer on this team, you'll have the opportunity to make a tangible difference in the lives of our congregation by handling administrative tasks, such as data entry, event planning, and facility management. Whether it's coordinating meetings, managing records, or providing general support, every role is crucial in helping us run smoothly and efficiently. By joining our Administrative Support team, you'll be part of a collaborative group that is dedicated to serving others with kindness, compassion, and attention to detail. Together, let's work together to provide top-notch administrative support that enables us to focus on what matters most: spreading the Gospel and making a positive impact in our community.

Production Design.

At S4C, our Production Design team is responsible for creating an immersive and engaging environment that sets the tone for our worship services, events, and community gatherings. As a volunteer on this team, you'll have the opportunity to bring your creative vision to life through set design, props, and other visual elements that help tell God's story. From designing stages to crafting decor, every detail matters in creating an atmosphere that inspires, uplifts, and connects our congregation. By joining our Production Design team, you'll be part of a collaborative group that is passionate about using art and creativity to honor God and serve others. Together, let's work together to design spaces that spark imagination, ignite passion, and point people to the heart of God.

Technical & Stage Management.

At S4C, our Technical & Stage Management team is responsible for setting the stage for our worship services, ensuring that every aspect of the experience runs smoothly and effectively. As a volunteer on this team, you'll play a crucial role in bringing our messages to life through lighting, sound, and visual effects. From adjusting lights to operating cameras, your technical expertise will help create an immersive environment that engages and inspires our congregation. By joining our Technical & Stage Management team, you'll have the opportunity to develop new skills, work with a talented group of individuals, and be part of a behind-the-scenes effort that enhances the overall worship experience. Together, let's work together to bring clarity, precision, and creativity to every service, creating an atmosphere that honors God and draws people deeper into His presence.

Communications.

At S4C, our Communications team is dedicated to sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ with our community, spreading hope and inspiration through various channels. As a volunteer on this team, you'll have the opportunity to play a vital role in helping us reach more people, both locally and globally, through social media, email, events, and other communications efforts. Whether it's crafting compelling content, creating engaging graphics, or managing our online presence, every contribution counts and helps us fulfill our mission. By joining our Communications team, you'll be part of a dynamic group that is passionate about using technology to serve God's people and share His love with the world. Together, let's work together to create connections, inspire lives, and build a community of faith through effective communication.
