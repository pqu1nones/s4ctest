Are you Ready? We encourage you to pray this prayer of repentace and receive salvation through Jesus Christ.

I'm ready to receive salvation through Jesus Christ. Dear Heavenly Father, in the name of Jesus, I thank you for sending your Son to die on the cross for my sins.

I believe that Jesus came to earth as God in human form and lived, was crucified, died, and rose from the dead. These events are proof that Jesus is indeed God in flesh.

I accept Jesus as my Lord and Savior, and I now confess my sins to you alone. I repent for all the wrongs I have done and ask for forgiveness. I am turning away from dishonoring and disrespecting the life and death of my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

I want to be a part of Your Royal Kingdom. Please forgive me, accept me as your child, and be my Lord and Savior. Amen!
