Are you Ready? We encourage you to pray this prayer of repentace and receive salvation through Jesus Christ.

Father God, in the name of Jesus, thank you for your Son, Jesus Christ, who died on the Cross because of my sins and the sins of this world. I believe that Jesus Christ came to earth, The Holy God in the form of a man, lived, was crucified, hung on a cross, died and was buried, rose up on the third day and that same Jesus Christ was God in the flesh. I believe that Jesus Christ did all this for ME, and I accept Jesus Christ as MY Lord and Savior. I am confessing with my mouth, and I believe in my heart that He truly is God. I confess my sins to you and you alone, and I ask for forgiveness. I repent for all the wrong I have done and for dishonoring and disrespecting the Life and Death of my Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. I want to be part of the Royal Kingdom of God. Please forgive and accept me and be my Lord and Savior. Amen.
