CROWNED / Women's Ministry

She is clothed with strength and dignity; she can laugh at the days to come. She speaks with wisdom, and faithful instruction is on her tongue.

Proverbs 31:25-26 NIV

Ladies, you are invited to join CROWNED, our church's vibrant women's ministry, where faith, fellowship, and encouragement come together! Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, meaningful connections, or simply a place to be uplifted, CROWNED is here for you. Through prayer, Bible study, and heartfelt conversations, we support one another in our journey as daughters of the King. No matter where you are in life, you belong here, surrounded by sisters who will walk with you in faith and love. Come and be CROWNED in His grace—your place is waiting!
