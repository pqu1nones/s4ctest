Welcome to S4C's Video Recordings and Sermon Notes Online Repository! You'll find a wealth of pastor-led teachings from our weekly worship services here. We aim not to replace scripture but to complement it, inspiring you to explore God's Word more deeply. We invite you to watch the videos, download the notes, share them with a friend or family member, and reflect on the life-changing truths we've uncovered together.

Visit our YouTube channel to experience our pastor's sermons in action. There, you'll find a wealth of other teaching resources. We encourage you to subscribe and join us on this journey of discovery and growth. May these resources catalyze your spiritual exploration, and may God's Word profoundly transform your life.
