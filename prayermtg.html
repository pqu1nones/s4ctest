Prayer Meeting

..do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God.

Philippians 4:6 ESV

Join us at our Prayer Meeting, where we'll gather together to seek God's presence and guidance in these uncertain times. This special evening will be filled with powerful intercession, heartfelt worship, and uplifting fellowship, allowing us to deepen our connection with the One who loves us most. As we come together in faith, we will seek His peace, wisdom, and strength—not only for ourselves but also for those around us. Whether you are in need of comfort, encouragement, or simply a time to rest in His presence, this meeting promises to be a transformative experience of connection, healing, and renewal. Don't miss this opportunity to be refreshed and inspired as we stand together in prayer and unity.
