February Fast 2025

We are excited to be on this journey with you over the next 21 days. Fasting is essential for the believer's continual victory. In Matthew 4:4, Jesus said that man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceeds out of the mouth of God. As we fast, we carve more space to hear God speak and see Him move. We are blazing a spiritual trail from heaven to earth. This is about growing and strengthening our relationship with God.

As we face ever-changing times, we find comfort in our God who does not change. (For I am the Lord, I do not change, Malachi 3:6a). As you are fasting, may you find peace at the master's feet. May your joy be complete in Christ, and may you grow in His Holy Spirit, being a light to those in darkness.

If you have not begun to prepare, it is not too late. Start today. Plan your fast. Define your purpose. Clear out space and time to have intimate times with the Lord.
