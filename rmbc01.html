Welcome to Rashon Messianic Bible College

Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

2 Timothy 2:15 KJV

Rashon Messianic Bible College

Paul's exhortation to Timothy in 2 Timothy 2:15 is a powerful reminder of the importance of staying focused and persevering in our faith. As we navigate the challenges of life, it's easy to get sidetracked by distractions and lose sight of what's truly important. But Paul encourages us to "rightly teach" God's word, just as he did (2 Timothy 4:2). By staying committed to sound doctrine and teaching, we can maintain a clear and consistent faith that will guide us through life's ups and downs. With this steady foundation, we'll be able to stand firm against the pressures of our world and shine like beacons of hope in the darkness.
