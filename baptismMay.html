May Water Baptism

You're Invited to Witness the Power of Community and the Joy of Redemption!

We are excited to invite you to join us as we celebrate the gift of water and the joy of redemption with two (2) individuals who have made the decision to follow Jesus Christ!

On May 17, 2025, at 1:00 PM (PDT), our church will be baptizing a group of brothers and sisters who are committed to living out their faith in new ways. We would be honored if you could join us to witness this powerful moment of spiritual transformation.

As we gather together, we'll celebrate the journey these individuals have been on, from discovering God's love to making a public declaration of their faith. This group baptism is a reminder that our walk with Christ is not alone, but with the support and encouragement of a community of believers.

We'll have worship music, preaching, and an opportunity to celebrate with our brothers and sisters in Christ. Refreshments will be provided following the baptism, where we'll share in fellowship and support one another.

Please arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the service to get settled. We'll also have a special prayer time before the baptisms, asking God to pour out His Spirit on this group of new believers.

RSVP: For reservation, contact Pastor Aundria using Water Baptism in the subject line.
