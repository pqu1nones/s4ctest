We Are Soldiers for Christ

He said to them, “Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.

Mark 16:15 NIV

At the heart of our community is a deep love for God and a passion for serving others. Our church is more than just a building - it's a family of believers who are called to live out their faith in everyday life. We invite you to join us as we strive to be a place where people from all walks of life can come together, find community, and discover the transformative power of the Lord Jesus Christ. We hope to share with you our mission and our believes - and introduce you to our church leaders.

Our Mission.

We are dedicated to promoting the gospel of Jesus Christ by any means necessary and, teaching every believer to walk by faith and not by sight as it is written in 2nd Corinthians 5:7

Our mission is to serve our surrounding as well as global communities as an educational and development resource organization, primarily reaching out to those who have lost their way or have been wounded in battle and are in need of healing or to be redirected back to the loving arms of Christ Jesus.

There are no color lines or barriers, no race preferences or prejudice. No age or sex discrimination, just come as you are, Matthew 16:24 our mission is to recruit, educate, train and lead by example.

Our Vision.

By the grace of God, we will produce a balanced people, co-laboring in a unified and balance pursuit of the Great Commission of our Lord, Jesus Christ. While targeting a multi-ethnic, multi-cultural people, we desire to effectively minister to a diverse social, economic, and ethnic background of people. We will unite them with Kingdom Love and Purpose by preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom of God and the soon coming of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, coupled with the teachings of the "Uncompromised" Word of God.

We will employ traditional, nontraditional, and progressive methods that include, but are not limited to, weekly worship services, crusades, evangelism, multi media ministry, and creative outreaches for children, teens, and young adults. We will endeavor to share our victorious lives in Christ Jesus with an unregenerate and perishing world. Our energies and resources will focus on developing and harvesting a healing oasis, empowering and encourage every believer within our reach, not just those of S4C but whomever Christ places in our path to be living witnesses of Christ's Resurrection, Rapture and soon return.

What We Believe.

One true and Living God.

That there is but one true and living God: maker of heaven and earth and all that is in them, Alpha and Omega (the beginning the end) who ever was, and is and shall be, time without end Amen. He is infinitely holy, mighty, tender, loving and glorious. Worthy of all possible love and honor, confidence, and obedience, majesty, dominion, both now and forever, and that in the unity of the Godhead there are three equal in every divine perfection, executing distinct but harmonious offices in the great work of redemption.

Jesus Christ

Co-exist and co-eternal with the Father, who, conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary, took upon Himself the form of man, bore our sins, carried our sorrows, and by the shedding of His precious blood upon the cross of Calvary, purchased redemption for all that would believe in Him. Then, bursting the bonds of death and hell rose from the grave and ascended on high, leading captivity captive, who was the great mediator between God and man. He stands at the right hand of God the Father, making intercession for those whom he laid down His life.

The Holy Spirit

The third person of the Godhead is the Spirit of the Father. Omnipotent, Omnipresent, performing an inexpressibly critical mission upon earth, convicting sin, righteousness, and judgment, drawing sinners to the Savior, rebuking, pleading, searching, comforting, guiding, quickening (to come or return to life), teaching, glorifying, baptizing and enduing (to provide with some quality or trait) with power from on high those who yield to His tender ministrations, preparing them for the great day of the Lord’s appearing..

The Fall of Man

We believe that man was created in the image of God, before whom he walked in holiness and purity, but that by voluntary disobedience and transgression (violation of a law, rule, or command), he fell from the Eden of purity and innocence to the depths of sin and iniquity (wickedness, sinfulness, grossly immoral acts,), and that in consequence of this, all mankind are sinners sold unto Satan. Sinners, not by constraint but by choice, are shaped in iniquity and utterly void by nature of that holiness required by the law of God. Positively inclined to evil, quality and without excuse justly deserving the condemnation of a just and holy God.

The Rapture of the Church, The Bride of Jesus Christ.

The rapture refers to the "being caught up" referred to in 1 Thessalonians 4:17, when the "dead in Christ" and "we who are alive and remain" will be caught up in the clouds to meet the Lord.


Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We believe that our identity is rooted in Jesus Christ. We are called to reflect His character, live out His love, and demonstrate His presence in our world. We strive to see the Church as a community of believers who are deeply rooted in their faith and passionate about living out the Gospel.

Church Is Family

We are a family on a mission to know, love, and serve Jesus together. As a church, we value community, connection, and belonging. We believe that every person has a unique role to play in our collective life, and we seek to create an environment where everyone can grow, learn, and flourish.

Kingdom Culture

We are called to live out the values of the Kingdom of God in all aspects of life. This means embracing a culture that is marked by humility, compassion, and justice. We strive to see the world through the lens of Jesus' teachings, and we seek to be a reflection of His love, mercy, and truth.

Spirit Led Life

We believe that the Holy Spirit is our guide, our strength, and our motivation. We seek to live a life that is surrendered to God's will, guided by the Holy Spirit, and empowered by faith. We strive to see each other living out their faith in practical ways, demonstrating the love of Christ to those around us.
