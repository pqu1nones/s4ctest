Welcome to Rashon Messianic Bible College

Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

2 Timothy 2:15 KJV

Rashon Messianic Bible College

Paul's exhortation to Timothy in 2 Timothy 2:15 is a powerful reminder of the importance of staying focused and persevering in our faith. As we navigate the challenges of life, it's easy to get sidetracked by distractions and lose sight of what's truly important. But Paul encourages us to "rightly teach" God's word, just as he did (2 Timothy 4:2). By staying committed to sound doctrine and teaching, we can maintain a clear and consistent faith that will guide us through life's ups and downs. With this steady foundation, we'll be able to stand firm against the pressures of our world and shine like beacons of hope in the darkness.

As the oldest living language on earth, Hebrew has been a cornerstone of Jewish culture and identity for millennia. With its rich history, complex grammar, and nuanced nuances, studying Hebrew can reveal profound insights into the biblical text, the Talmud (ancient Jewish law code), and the lives of millions of Jews around the world. As an associate degree in Hebrew program, we invite you to embark on a journey that will not only deepen your understanding of Jewish scripture and tradition but also foster a deeper appreciation for the linguistic and cultural heritage of Israel. Through our curriculum, which combines coursework in biblical studies, language skills, and cultural immersion, you will gain a solid foundation in modern Hebrew reading and writing, as well as explore the historical, literary, and spiritual contexts that have shaped this vibrant language over time.
